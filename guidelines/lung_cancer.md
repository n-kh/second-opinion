# NCCN Clinical Practice Guidelines in Oncology: Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (v3.2026)

## 1. Staging Workup for Advanced Disease (Stage IV)
- Histological confirmation (adenocarcinoma vs. squamous cell).
- Molecular testing is mandatory for adenocarcinoma: EGFR, ALK, ROS1, RET, MET, BRAF, NTRK, and PD-L1 expression.
- Brain MRI is highly recommended for all Stage IV patients to evaluate for silent central nervous system (CNS) metastases.

## 2. EGFR Mutation-Positive Metastatic NSCLC (Stage IV)
- Standard of Care: First-line tyrosine kinase inhibitor (TKI) therapy (Osimertinib is the preferred category 1 recommendation).
- Platinum-doublet chemotherapy is NOT recommended as first-line therapy unless EGFR TKIs are contraindicated.
- Next steps: Brain MRI if not done, monitor treatment response with chest/abdomen CT every 8-12 weeks.
